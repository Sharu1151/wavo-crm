# Investor Pitch Summary - Wavo CRM

This document outlines Wavo CRM's 10-slide investor deck, designed to raise a seed funding round for product development, sales, and infrastructure scaling.

---

## Slide 1: Title Slide (The Hook)
*   **Visual Design:** Clean dark theme with a glowing indigo logo. Minimalist layout.
*   **Header:** **Wavo CRM**
*   **Sub-Header:** Turn Conversations Into Customers.
*   **Core Message:** The modern WhatsApp Business Helper and CRM built to help small and medium businesses scale operations, automate follow-ups, and convert chats into revenue.

---

## Slide 2: The Problem (The Market Gap)
*   **Visual Design:** Pain points listed alongside a screenshot showing cluttered spreadsheets and unread WhatsApp screens.
*   **Content:**
    *   **The Chat Conundrum:** SMBs close deals in WhatsApp, but manage pipelines in static notebooks or spreadsheets.
    *   **The Bleeding Funnel:** 62% of inbound messaging leads are lost due to delayed follow-ups and lack of tracking.
    *   **Overwhelming Scale:** Business owners cannot write custom scripts or pay enterprise prices for complex CRMs like Salesforce.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (Introducing Wavo CRM)
*   **Visual Design:** A side-by-side view showcasing an incoming WhatsApp message translating to a pipeline change in Wavo's clean dashboard.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Unified CRM Workspace:** Connects directly to the official WhatsApp Business Cloud API.
    *   **AI Outreach Co-Pilot:** Scores leads and drafts custom replies in real-time.
    *   **Automated Campaigns:** Broadcasts promotions and reminders without spam blocks.

---

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity (TAM, SAM, SOM)
*   **Visual Design:** Concentric circles mapping target markets.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $24B conversational CRM market.
    *   **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $4.8B SMB market in regions with high WhatsApp usage (LATAM, SEA, India, EU).
    *   **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** $180M target (capturing 3% market share in year 5).

---

## Slide 5: The Product (Seamless Core Features)
*   **Visual Design:** Icons showcasing core capabilities.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Kanban Sales Pipeline:** Drag-and-drop lead stages.
    *   **WhatsApp Broadcasting:** Bulk template delivery via background message queues.
    *   **AI Capabilities:** One-click summary of customer histories and automated response drafts.

---

## Slide 6: Business & Subscription Model
*   **Visual Design:** Clean table outlining plans.
*   **Content:**
    *   **High Margin SaaS:** Pro Plan ($29/mo) and Business Plan ($79/mo) drive recurring revenue.
    *   **Conversation-Based Add-ons:** Pay-as-you-go credit billing for high-volume messaging.
    *   **Strong Unit Economics:** Gross profit margin > 80% with low server footprints.

---

## Slide 7: Market Traction (Growth Indicators)
*   **Visual Design:** Line chart showing month-over-month ARR growth.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Beta Phase Success:** 250 active businesses onboarded during private preview.
    *   **Customer Engagement:** Over 1.2M messages processed, with an average open rate of 88%.
    *   **Retention:** 96% client retention month-over-month.

---

## Slide 8: Go-To-Market Strategy
*   **Visual Design:** Acquisition funnel graphic.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Meta Ads Funnel:** Click-to-WhatsApp ads direct users to our automated signup bot.
    *   **App Directory Integrations:** Instant visibility on Shopify, Make, and Zapier.
    *   **Educational SEO:** Content hubs addressing WhatsApp customer support challenges.

---

## Slide 9: Competitive Landscape
*   **Visual Design:** Competitive matrix comparison.
*   **Content:**
    *   **HubSpot/Salesforce:** Expensive, complex setup, weak out-of-the-box WhatsApp integrations.
    *   **Twilio Links:** Requires custom developers and technical setups.
    *   **Wavo CRM Advantage:** Zero-code setup, mobile-first design, built-in AI, and affordable SMB pricing.

---

## Slide 10: The Ask (Raise Details)
*   **Visual Design:** Donut chart showing budget allocation.
*   **Header:** **Raising $1.5M Seed Round**
*   **Content:**
    *   **55% Product Development:** Accelerating AI features and mobile app integrations.
    *   **30% Sales & Marketing:** Direct ads spend and integration channel listing.
    *   **15% Operations & Security:** Compliance certification and database infrastructure scaling.
