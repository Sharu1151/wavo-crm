# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy - Wavo CRM

This document details the market positioning, buyer personas, customer acquisition channels, launch timelines, and growth KPIs for Wavo CRM.

---

## 1. Value Proposition & Market Positioning

Wavo CRM addresses a critical bottleneck: **SMBs interact with customers on WhatsApp, but manage their pipeline inside spreadsheets or static notebooks.** This disconnect results in lost leads, forgotton follow-ups, and flatlining conversion rates.

### 1.1 Messaging Matrix

| Audience | Problem | Wavo CRM Solution | Value Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Real Estate Agents** | Leads drop off due to delayed replies to property inquiries. | Inbound webhooks capture lead context, and AI suggests property-specific responses. | 5x faster lead response times |
| **Clinics & Salons** | High no-show rates for appointments. | Automated WhatsApp templates dispatch payment and booking reminders. | 45% reduction in appointment no-shows |
| **Retail & E-commerce** | Cart abandonment and one-time buyers. | Segmented broadcast promotions targeted at historical buyers. | 22% increase in customer lifetime value (LTV) |
| **Sales Teams** | Reps lose track of tasks and follow-up schedules. | Unified Kanban pipeline view, automated calendar alerts, and task tracking. | 35% higher close-rates per rep |

## 2. Target Customer Segments & Personas
Wavo's initial expansion strategy prioritizes **highly conversational SMBs** operating in regions with deep WhatsApp penetration (e.g., India, Brazil, LATAM, SEA, and parts of Europe):
1.  **Professional Service Providers:** Insurance agents, tuition centers, clinics, mortgage brokers.
2.  **Local Businesses:** Retail stores, custom manufacturers, event planners.
3.  **Digital Teams:** Freelance hubs, remote agencies, e-commerce brands.

### 3.1 Paid Meta Advertising (Click-to-WhatsApp Funnel)
*   **Strategy:** Run Facebook/Instagram ads containing a "Send Message" CTA. Clicking the ad launches a WhatsApp chat thread with Wavo's automated onboarding bot.
*   **Onboarding Integration:** The bot automatically registers their phone number, qualifies their business type, and generates their Wavo tenant login link.

### 3.2 Content Marketing & SEO
*   **Focus:** Target long-tail search queries (e.g., "how to send automatic invoice reminders on WhatsApp", "best CRM for insurance agents using WhatsApp").
*   **Assets:** In-depth guides, free template files, and video walkthroughs of customer-retention strategies.

### 3.3 Integration Partnerships
*   List Wavo CRM on popular directories and application hubs: **Shopify App Store**, **Zapier App Directory**, and **Make.com integrations**.
*   This positions Wavo CRM as the top sync tool for small businesses running store logistics elsewhere.

## 4. Onboarding & Customer Retention
*   **Interactive Setup Wizard:** A step-by-step walkthrough guides new users through setting up their business profile, configuring their first customer tag, and executing a test template message in less than 5 minutes.
*   **Retention Drip Campaigns:** If a user registers but doesn't log a customer interaction within 48 hours, they receive an email tutorial highlighting the "Lead Capture Webhook" feature.
*   **Premium SLA Support:** Paid tiers unlock 24/7 dedicated support channels (ironically, hosted inside a WhatsApp support group).

## 5. Key Business Metrics & KPIs
To evaluate the success of the GTM rollout, the management dashboard monitors:
1.  **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Total sales and marketing spend divided by new customer accounts. Target: < $40 on Pro Plan.
2.  **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) & Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):** SaaS subscription progression.
3.  **Net Churn Rate:** Percentage of revenue lost from subscription cancellations. Target: < 4% monthly.
4.  **Lead-to-Won Conversion Velocity:** The average number of days it takes for a new lead to transition to a Closed Won stage.
5.  **Average WhatsApp Message Open Rate:** Metric demonstrating campaign efficiency for businesses. Target: > 85% open rate.
